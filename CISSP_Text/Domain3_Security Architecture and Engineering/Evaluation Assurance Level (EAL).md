
The evaluation assurance level is an indicator of how rigorously a system has been tested based on the international standard, Common Criteria.
The higher the level, the greater the degree of security assurance.

EAL1: Functional testing
EAL2: Structured testing
EAL3: Systematic testing and checking
EAL4: Systematic design, testing, and review
EAL5: Semiformal design and testing
EAL6: Semiformally verified design and testing
EAL7: Formal verified design and testing

評価保証レベルは、システムが国際標準規格であるコモンクライテリアに基づいて、どの程度厳密にテストされたかを示す指標です。
