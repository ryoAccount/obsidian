
M of N control, also known as "quorum authentication," is a mechanism that requires approval from multiple people when performing an operation.
"M" represents the number of people required for approval, and "N" represents the total number of people.

M of N 制御は「クォーラム認証」とも呼ばれ、操作をする際に複数人の承認が必要になる仕組みです。 
「M」は承認に必要な人数、「N」は全体の人数を表しています。
