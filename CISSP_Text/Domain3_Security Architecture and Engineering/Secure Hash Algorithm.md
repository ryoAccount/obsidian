
The Secure Hash Algorithm (SHA) has been designated the standard hash function by the [[National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)]].
SHA-2 and SHA-3 are recommended.
SHA-0, SHA-1, MD5, and HAVAL are not recommended due to their vulnerability to collision attacks.

SHA-2、SHA-3を推奨。
SHA-0、SHA-1、MD5、HAVALは、衝突攻撃に対する脆弱性が指摘されたため非推奨。
